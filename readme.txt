Social Share Bar provides your the feature to add your social links to your site with multiple options.

== Description ==

Social Share Bar provides your social links to site front-end. It helps your customers and fans connect to your other social networks.

Major features in Social Share Bar:

* Display your social links to every page of your site.
* Multiple options to display social links.
* Three designs to choose form. 

== Installation ==

Upload the Social Share plugin to your blog, activate it, and then enter your social links on its dashboard.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
